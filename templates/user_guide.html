User Guide for the Social Annotation Platform

Welcome to the Social Annotation Platform! This guide will walk you through everything you need to know about using the platform for weekly reading discussions.

1. Accessing the Weekly Reading

Each week, you will receive a copy of the reading material on MyeLearning. Ensure you review it before participating in the annotation activity.

2. Accessing the Annotation Activity

You will find a dedicated link to the annotation activity on MyeLearning. This will take you to the platform where you can view the PDF and add your comments.

3. Logging In with Your Registered Name

When prompted, enter your registered name as it appears in the course registration. Your participation points will be linked to this name.

4. Navigating the PDF

  • Use the "Next Page" and "Previous Page" buttons to navigate through the reading.
  • Ensure you are commenting on the correct page to keep discussions relevant.

5. Creating Comments

  • Click the "Add Comment" button.
  • Click on the PDF to place your comment.
  • Type your comment in the box that appears. Remember, comments must be between 25-150 words.

6. Deleting Comments

You can delete your own comments by clicking the "Delete" button and confirming your name.

7. Etiquette for Commenting

Follow these guidelines for respectful and constructive discussions:

  • Avoid overlapping comments with others’ comments.
  • Be respectful and constructive in your comments.

8. Using the Email Function

Optionally, you can provide your email when submitting a comment to receive a copy of it for your records.

9. Keeping a Personal Record

It’s recommended to keep a personal record of your comments for easy reference and to prevent loss of any information.

10. Final Reminders

Ensure you participate on time, and if you encounter any issues, reach out to your instructor or TA for assistance.

Happy annotating!
